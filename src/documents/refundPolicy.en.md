Last updated: June 11, 2026

# 14-day, no-questions-asked refund

You may request a **full refund within 14 days** of:

- Your initial purchase of Freemap Premium, or
- each automatic renewal of Your Subscription.

Within this 14-day window, **no reason is required** — simply contact Us or Paddle and We will refund the amount paid.

After a refund is issued, Your Freemap Premium access ends and Your account reverts to the free tier.

# Right of withdrawal (EU consumers)

If You are a consumer in the European Union, You have a statutory right to withdraw from the purchase of digital content and services within 14 days. The no-questions-asked window described above meets or exceeds that right. Our 14-day refund applies regardless of whether You have already started using the Premium features.

# After the 14-day window

Once 14 days have passed since a purchase or renewal, that payment is generally **non-refundable**, because the Subscription has been made available to You for the period concerned.

This does not affect Your mandatory statutory rights. In particular, You may still be entitled to a remedy (including a refund) if the Service is faulty, not as described, or otherwise not in conformity with these Terms or applicable consumer-protection law. We may also grant refunds outside the standard window at Our discretion, for example on a pro-rata basis where We discontinue a Subscription through no fault of Yours.

# Credits

Credits are a prepaid balance used for consumption-based features (currently offline map exports). For Credits, the 14-day refund applies to the **unused** balance: within 14 days of a Credit purchase You may request a refund of any Credits You have not yet spent. Credits that have already been spent are **not refundable**, because the corresponding digital content has already been delivered. Credits do not expire.

# How to request a refund

To request a refund, please use either of the following:

- **Email Us** at freemap@freemap.sk with the email address used for the purchase and, if available, the Paddle order or invoice number; or
- **Use the billing-management or receipt link** in Your Paddle purchase or renewal email, which lets You contact Paddle support directly.

We aim to acknowledge refund requests within a few business days. Once a refund is approved, Paddle processes it to Your original payment method; the time for the funds to appear depends on Your bank or card issuer.

# Cancelling future renewals

A refund for a past payment is separate from cancelling future renewals. To stop Your Subscription from renewing, cancel it as described in the [Terms of Service](#document=termsOfService). Cancelling stops future charges but does not, by itself, refund a payment already made — use the refund process above for that.
