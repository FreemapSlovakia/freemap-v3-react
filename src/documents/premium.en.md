# Freemap Premium and Credits

Freemap.sk is an open web and mobile mapping application for outdoor and everyday navigation, built largely on open data including OpenStreetMap, with map browsing, search, routing, and drawing and export tools.

**Ing. Martin Ždila s.r.o.** sells two optional paid products that enhance the application: the **Freemap Premium** subscription and prepaid **Credits** (see the company details at the bottom of this page).

# Freemap Premium

**Freemap Premium** is a yearly subscription that unlocks:

- **No advertising** — the ad banner is removed.
- **Premium map layers** — Strava Heatmap, hi-res detailed shading of Slovakia and Czechia, the highest zoom levels of the Outdoor Map and of the orthophoto maps of Slovakia and Czechia, and various WMS-based maps.
- **Premium photos** — access to premium photographs shown on the map.
- **Multimodal routing** — route planning combining multiple modes of transport.

## Price

**EUR 8 per year.** The subscription gives you one year of premium access and renews yearly unless cancelled.

You can manage and cancel your subscription at any time; cancellation takes effect at the end of the paid period. For details on refunds, see our [Refund Policy](#document=refundPolicy).

# Credits

**Credits** are a prepaid balance you can spend on consumption-based features. They are currently used for **offline map exports**, where each export consumes a number of credits depending on the map type and the number of map tiles exported. We may make credits usable for additional services in the future (for example, cached offline maps).

## Price

Credits are sold in an amount of your choice, with a **minimum purchase of 500 credits for EUR 5**. Credits do not expire and have no cash value. Unused credits can be refunded within 14 days of purchase — see our [Refund Policy](#document=refundPolicy).

# How to get them

You purchase Freemap Premium or Credits directly inside the Freemap.sk application after signing in, using the premium / credits options in the menu. Payments are processed securely by our payment provider; prices include applicable VAT and we issue you a receipt or invoice.
